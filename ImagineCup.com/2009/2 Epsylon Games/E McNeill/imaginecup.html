In July 2009 I attended the World Finals of Microsoft's Imagine Cup competition in the game development category. My game, Alternex, won second place.

It was pretty incredible to get flown to Cairo and get to hang out in a foreign city with a multinational crowd of genius tech/art people. It's probably the coolest thing that's ever happened to me in my pre-professional life.

One of Microsoft's bloggers at the event did a series of posts following me through the competition. That story was published during the competition on the Imagine Cup blog and talks all about the details of the event.

I also got a swanky news release from my college and a story in the student newspaper, The Dartmouth.
