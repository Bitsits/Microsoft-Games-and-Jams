Art

To start with, I am not an artist. But I did take a class on Digital Arts, and I had a lot of fun, so I figured I would share my creations. All of the following were created in Maya.

First, the biped. This was our final project in 3D Digital Modeling. He's some sort of cyber ninja. I don't really know. But I like him!

I also made a 10-second animation, which was really very rewarding. Download it.

This spider was made using subdivision surfaces. He was rigged, but never animated, and never textured.

This room was my first ever project in 3D Modeling. It's designed to be the ultimate sunroom. Note that the far wall is just one big mirror (most of the objects in the first image are reflections). Alas, the fancy lighting that I set up for this scene bugged out on me at the last minute, but I'm still satisfied with the result.
