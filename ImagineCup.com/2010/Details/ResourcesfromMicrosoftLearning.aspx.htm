Microsoft Learning Resources


Microsoft Learning provides the tools to empower you to fulfill your potential and reach your learning goals. We offer a diverse range of programs that includes Microsoft training and certification, Microsoft Press books, skills assessments, classroom training, and e-learning solutions. All are designed to serve your needs to help you get the career you’ve always wanted.

Student-Priced Certification Exams and a Second Shot at Passing Your Exam (if you need one)!*


Today's students, like you, are looking for ways to build skills that differentiate them from others and open the doors to tomorrow's jobs. Recent IDC research indicates that the number of IT jobs worldwide will rise by 5.8 million between the end of 2009 and the end of 2013. Many students are taking advantage of opportunities to get skilled in technology.
 
 
Certification Exams at Student Prices
 
In support, Microsoft Learning announced student-price certification exams to help you earn Microsoft certifications that will differentiate you in your career. Student prices, of up to 55 percent discount off the standard price, are available on Microsoft technologies. Review the full list of available student certifications.  Start Now
 
 
Student Second Shot Vouchers
 
Great news! Now you can get two shots at passing a Microsoft Certification exam in the student series (exams that have a 072 prefix). To take advantage of this offer, you must take your first certification exam and free retake exam between January 13, 2010, and June 30, 2010. Register now and get a voucher for your exam!
