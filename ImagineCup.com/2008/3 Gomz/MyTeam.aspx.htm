Round Entry
Team Gomz.zip

Platform : XBOX360 / PC -> We strongly recommend XBOX360 Platform.

To preserve the environment by using the technology can mean that we can resolve the problem through the technology. Our team got to know that most pollutants are produced in the process of converting resources into energy.

Therefore we created this game under the assumption that pollutants can be turned into renewable energy.

In the world of the game, there are nanomachine named "Cleaner" who is in charge of converting pollutants into energy. However cleaners are not capable of moving themselves ,so somebody has to move them.

 This job is done by the player. The player is an artificial life and cleans up the polluted areas. Player move around designated areas within limited time and collect cleaners and distribute energy to needed area and upgrade their own components.

 Also player can give help other players and educate not to release pollutants. the pollution-free environment can be sustained only when people raise awareness of serious pollution and energy is distributed fairly.

The CLEANUP wanted to let people understand this message through this game.

Now CLEANUP can be played in the level of tutorial with the background of a small two-story house. We will make players use all the functions of upgrade and play the game in the polluted background.   At the same time it will be possible to co-play the game through distribution of energy am.

We will also show more elaborate quality in visual part.
