City Rain.zip
City Rain is a 2D isometric pixel art game. It mixes a creative puzzle engine with an urban planning simulation, focusing on ecology and environmental sustainability. You play as the mayor of a virtual city, deciding on the best way to build a profitable but also ecologically sustainable city.
CityRain.zip

Imagine a World where everybody was aware about environmental issues and respected our Planet.

City Rain provides this world to the players by teaching them precisous lessons about:
Urbanism
Ecology
Sustainable Environment.

City Rain can be used as a teaching tool, helping make K-12 students aware of environmental issues.
Because the game has several difficulty levels, it can be slow and smooth so the players can learn while their
rythm is respected, or fast and challenging so the players can test their skills and compete with their friends.

With the XNA Framework technology provided by Microsoft we can teach in the best way: where the students learn having fun. Mother Gaia Studio wishes to make youth aware of how to make ours a better planet.

Enjoy
