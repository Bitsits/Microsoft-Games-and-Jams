HERO OF THE WEEK
Jake Poznanski &
Sam Kaufmann
Jake and Sam wanted to be a part of something new and saw a lot of potential in developing for Windows Phone 7. They figured they could be the first to write games they knew users had experienced before and would want for Windows Phone 7. The free DreamSpark access and Microsoft pubCenter sealed the deal.

As computer science majors, Jake and Sam were already capable of building great apps. They hired a graphic designer to give the games extra polish and the effort paid off! Every day in the high score table they see tens of thousands of users competing for the top score in Word Search. Their best feedback so far? “…This game is awesome. Really, it’s infinitely more glorified because of the high score system.”

Jake and Sam have enjoyed the development experience from start to finish. They appreciate the ease of developing and testing apps on the Visual Studio-based SDK and that the XNA toolkit and Microsoft pubCenter are so flexible – they give these developers maximum control over details and which ads their users see.

It’s no surprise, then, that their superpower of choice would be the “Inception”-like ability to control people’s dreams! They seem to be ahead of the curve in other areas … so why not? We just ask that they keep developing for Windows Phone 7.
