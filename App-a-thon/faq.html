FAQ

Q. Who is eligible to participate in this promotion?
A. Participants must be a legal resident of the 50 United States (including the District of Columbia), 18 years of age or older, and current a full-time or part-time student enrolled in a U.S. college or university at the time of participation.

Q. What are the prizes for this promotion?
A. Each of the first one thousand (1,000) students who completes an Official Entry Form, including submitting the name of his or her approved app during the promotion period will receive one (1) Xbox 360® game (Approximate Retail Value: $50).

One student submitting the most approved apps during the promotion period will receive the Bonus Prize #1 of the recipient's choice of either (1) $5,000, awarded in the form of a corporate check payable to the Bonus Prize #1 recipient or (2) one (1) Windows Phone, a $200 bank gift card intended to be used for phone activation and/or service, one (1) 4GB Xbox 360® with Kinect™, and $4,000, awarded in the form of a corporate check payable to the Bonus Prize #1 recipient. Regardless of the Bonus Prize #1 chosen, the Bonus Prize #1 recipient will also receive $2,575.75, awarded in the form of a corporate check payable to the student, which is intended to assist with any federal, state, or other tax obligations related to the Bonus Prize #1.

Additionally, The three (3) students submitting the second, third, and fourth most approved apps during the promotion period will each receive the Bonus Prize #2 of $1,000, awarded in the form of a corporate check payable to the Bonus Prize #2 recipients. In addition, the Bonus Prize #2 recipients will also receive $515.15, awarded in the form of a corporate check payable to the student, which is intended to assist with any federal, state, or other tax obligations related to the Bonus Prize #2.

Q. How will I know if I will get one of the prizes?
A. The one thousand (1,000) students will be shipped their Xbox 360® game within approximately eight (8) weeks following the promotion period to the address associated with their entry.

The Bonus Prize recipients will be notified by phone/mail to the phone number/address associated with their entry approximately seven (7) calendar days after the conclusion of the promotion period.

Q. How long is this promotion going on?
A. Offer begins at 11:59 p.m. PT on April 11, 2011 and ends at 11:59 p.m. PT on June 30, 2011 or until the supply of one thousand (1,000) prizes is exhausted, whichever occurs first.

Q. What do I need to do to enter?
A. First, register for an App Hub membership as a student at https://users.create.msdn.com/Register/ through DreamSpark. Registering with a DreamSpark account will allow the $99 fee to be waived. Secondly, download the free developer tools to get started. Next, create as many Windows Phone apps as you'd like for submission, approval and publishing to the Windows Phone Marketplace during the promotion period. Lastly, visit www.wpstudentapp.com and complete an Official Entry Form, including the name of your approved app during the promotion period.

Q. I have an approved app in the Windows Phone Marketplace that I submitted before the start of the promotion, can I use this for my entry?
A. No, only apps that are submitted to the Windows Phone Marketplace for approval during the promotional period are eligible for the Offer.

Q. Do I need a Windows Phone to develop and submit apps to Windows Phone marketplace?
A. You do not need a Windows Phone to participate in this campaign or to submit an app in marketplace.

Q. Do I have to pay $99 fee to become a member of AppHub?
A. The $99 fee is waived for students who are verified through Dreamspark.

Q. Why do I have to validate my identity with Geotrust?
See here: http://create.msdn.com/en-US/home/about/developer_registration_walkthrough_confirmation#mivo

Q. How do I verify my identity with Geotrust?
A. Go to App Hub and submit an app. You'll get an email from GeoTrust – check your junk filters if you don't get it within 24 hours. After you register your identity, Geo Trust will request a copy of one piece of identification that has your signature. Once you send in your signed identification your application will be approved within 5 business days. Once approved your app will appear in the market place within one day. If you need to speak to someone in Geotrust for ID Verification, please contact Geotrust directly, either via their instant chat line, or by emailing orderprocessing@geotrust.com
